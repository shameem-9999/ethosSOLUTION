About Us

Though a significant number of manufacturing industries has been established in last decade, Bangladesh is feeling an indigence of expertise to support the industries for smooth operation. Under this circumstance a school of expertise came under an umbrella for developing a expertise platform named “ethos SOLUTION” for ensuring smooth and quality services to industry. “ethos SOLUTION” has started it’s journey in 2014 .
